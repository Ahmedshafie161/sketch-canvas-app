## ğŸ› TROUBLESHOOTING

### Shapes won't resize
- Make sure **Select tool** is active
- Click shape to select it
- Look for **blue handles** at corners
- Drag handles to resize

### Animations not playing
- Make sure shapes have animation order set
- Click **Animate** button first
- Enter order number (1, 2, 3...)
- Then click **Play**

### Table editor not showing
- Click directly on the **table**
- Editor panel appears on right side
- Click outside table to close

### Can't add text to shape
- **Double-click** the shape (not single click)
- Type your text in prompt
- Press OK

### Dark mode not working
- Click the **Sun/Moon** button in toolbar
- Refresh page if needed
- Preference is saved automatically

### Drawings not converting correctly
- Draw shapes more clearly
- Circles: close the loop completely
- Triangles: make 3 distinct corners
- Squares: keep similar width/height
